What is Cerebrum?

Cerebrum is a all-in-one solution to centralize the latest advancements in natural language processing and generative AI within a single, intuitive editing platform.
It's designed to assist you in every step of your research, analysis and note-taking journey.

  • Centralized Knowledge Hub : Cerebrum allows you to consolidate vast and diverse sources of knowledge into powerful hubs, simplifying research and analysis.
  • Cutting-Edge AI Integration : Harness the latest generative AI tools to interact with your content intelligently and efficiently.
  • Creative Content Crafting: : Cerebrum's canvas simplifies the creation of a wide range of content, including text, visuals, PowerPoint presentations, and more.

Memory module : Where Insights Reside.

Memory is the foundation of Cerebrum, where your journey begins. This module serves as your knowledge repository, housing all your ingested information, research materials, and insights. It's the starting point for your intellectual endeavors, providing easy access to your accumulated wisdom.

Chat module : Speak with your brain !

The Chat Module acts as your conversational partner within Cerebrum. It enables you to interact with your stored knowledge, asking questions, seeking clarifications, and exploring ideas. This module leverages AI to facilitate dynamic and intelligent conversations, enhancing your ability to extract valuable insights from your Memory.

Chart module : Deepen the analysis.

The Chart Module takes your analysis to the next level. It allows you to extract, visualize, and interpret data from your Memory, making complex information more accessible. Whether you need to uncover trends, patterns, or correlations, this module empowers you to create informative charts and graphs, enhancing your analytical capabilities.

Note Panel : Craft Your Ideas into Reality

The Note Panel is your creative canvas within Cerebrum. Here, you transform the knowledge and insights gathered from Memory, Chat Module, and Chart Module into tangible content. It's where you compose articles, reports, presentations, and more. With intuitive editing and formatting tools, the Note Panel lets you shape your ideas and present them in a compelling manner, giving your work a professional edge.
